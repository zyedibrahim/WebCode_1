Facts Of Breweries

The Slavic word ‘beer’ came from the verb ‘to drink’. Initially, beer was any kind of drink. The earliest evidence of brewing beer dates back to about 3500-2900 B.C. Ancient evidence, in the form of chemical residues, was found in ancient Iran. The strongest beer in the world has a strength of 67.5%. It was created in 2017 by the Scottish brewery Brewmeister. The beer is called Snake Venom.

What’s MadTree Like? The MadTree taproom is massive, while still being warm and inviting. They have plenty of beer on tap, and don’t limit themselves to their core styles, also keeping a rotating lineup of fun taproom exclusives to try as well. The brewery has a massive beer garden (it’s the size of their entire first brewery and taproom) that is the perfect place to spend a summer evening with your friends and family enjoying a drink and a few games of corn hole. The biggest thing that you can count on is that MadTree prides themselves on trying to create a space – and products – that allow as many folks as possible to have exactly what they want.
